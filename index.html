Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central is a software as a service ERP solution for the mid-size market. It was referred to as “Dynamics 365 Tenerife” during development and is often called “NAV in the Cloud.”

Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central provides the same rich functionality as Microsoft Dynamics NAV — Microsoft’s flagship solution for the mid-size market that is used by over 160,000 companies worldwide. Both solutions offer tight integration with other Microsoft cloud-based solutions such as Microsoft Dynamics 365 for Sales, Microsoft Office 365, etc. Microsoft describes Business Central as: “An all-in-one business management solution that helps organizations streamline business processes, improve customer interactions and enable growth.”

Business Central vs. Microsoft Dynamics

Essentials maps 1:1 to the functionality provided in the Dynamics NAV Starter Pack, and Premium maps to the Dynamics NAV Extended Pack.
