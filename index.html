Is Business Central right for your business?

Key areas of comparison

Business Central

Key areas

Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central is a software
as a service ERP solution for the mid-size market.
It was referred to as “Dynamics 365 Tenerife” during
development and is often called “NAV in the Cloud.”

Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central provides the same rich functionality as
Microsoft Dynamics NAV — Microsoft’s flagship solution for the mid-size market that
is used by over 160,000 companies worldwide. Both solutions offer tight integration with
other Microsoft cloud-based solutions such as Microsoft Dynamics 365 for Sales,
Microsoft Office 365, etc. Microsoft describes Business Central as:
“An all-in-one business management solution that helps organizations
streamline business processes, improve customer interactions and enable growth.”

Business Central vs.
Microsoft Dynamics

Standard functionality

Just like Microsoft Dynamics NAV, Business Central
functionality is divided across two packs. Essentials maps 1:1
to the functionality provided in the Dynamics NAV
Starter Pack, and Premium maps to the Dynamics NAV
Extended Pack.
The table below shows the high-level functional areas that are
addressed by both solutions.

Business Central delivers 3 key business benefits:

  • - Business without silos
  • - Actionable insights
  • - Solutions built to evolve

Explained

1

Business without silos

Can be used as a central hub that connects with familiar office tools through workflow (Microsoft Flow) and task automation (Office 365)

2

Actionable insights

Delivered with Power BI integration and through Azure intelligence tools, such as Microsoft Cortana for forecasting

3

Solutions built to evolve

A flexible platform that can scale to handle increasing volumes and complexity through support for Microsoft’s extensibility model
